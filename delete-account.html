How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps inside the MeetEra app:

1
Open the MeetEra app and log in to your account.
2
Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
3
Tap the Settings icon (⚙️) in the top right corner.
4
Scroll down and tap Account.
5
Tap Delete Account and confirm your choice.
⚠️ This action is permanent

Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your profile (name, photo, bio, city)
  • All messages and conversations
  • Events you created or joined
  • Marketplace listings
  • Buddy connections
  • All other account data stored by MeetEra

Data Retention

Your data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion. Some anonymised aggregate data may be retained for legal compliance purposes.

Need help or can't access the app? Email us and we'll delete your account manually.
