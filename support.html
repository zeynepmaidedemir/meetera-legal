Frequently Asked Questions

What is MeetEra?
MeetEra is a social companion app for Erasmus students. Connect with buddies, discover events in your host city, join communities, and make the most of your time abroad.
Is MeetEra free?
Yes, MeetEra is free to download and use. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core experience will always be free.
How do I delete my account?
Open the app, go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. If you need help, email us.
How do I report someone?
Tap the menu icon on any profile or post and select "Report". Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours. For urgent safety concerns, contact us directly.
Does MeetEra handle marketplace transactions?
No. The marketplace is a listings board — buyers and sellers arrange payment and exchange directly. MeetEra is not responsible for any transactions.
Which devices are supported?
MeetEra runs on iOS 13.0+ and Android 8.0+. Make sure your app is updated for the best experience.
I forgot my password — what do I do?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email. We'll send you a reset link. If you signed up with Google or Apple, simply use the same option to log in again.
