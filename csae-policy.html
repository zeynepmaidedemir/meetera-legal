MeetEra — Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

Our Commitment

MeetEra is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form. The app is intended for users aged 18 and older.

Age Restriction

MeetEra is exclusively designed for users who are 18 years of age or older. Users are required to confirm their age during registration. Accounts found to belong to minors are immediately terminated.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on MeetEra:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or abuses minors
  • Any content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates child sexual abuse
  • Any grooming, solicitation, or trafficking of minors
  • Sharing, distributing, or linking to child sexual abuse material (CSAM)

Reporting and Enforcement

All users can report any content or account that violates these standards directly within the app. Reports are reviewed promptly. Violations result in immediate account suspension and, where required by law, reports to relevant authorities including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC).

How to Report

To report a violation, tap the menu icon on any profile or post within the app and select "Report". You may also contact us directly at zeynepmaidedemir@gmail.com.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us at zeynepmaidedemir@gmail.com.
